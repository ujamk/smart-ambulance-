## Week 2 Log (Development Started)

During Week 2, the group began the development phase of the Smart Ambulance System.

Each team member started working on their assigned component. I focused on developing the ambulance system, starting with basic functionality such as printing patient data and structuring the program.

Other team members began developing the patient monitor, hospital system, and database system. We agreed to keep the first version simple to ensure all components could run without errors.

We also created GitHub issues to track tasks and progress. Regular commits were made to document development progress.

We encountered minor challenges in understanding MQTT communication, but as a group, we agreed to research and implement it in the following week.

Overall, this week focused on building the foundation of the system and ensuring all members were contributing.
